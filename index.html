if you don't have a password manager, you really should. any of these will do:

  • keepassXC
  • 1password
  • lastpass
if you have no idea what to do, you should use:
  • english-eff dictionary
  • 4 words long
  • no word separator
  • Capitalized

if this is an important high-security password, you should use at least 6 words. for less important passwords, 4 words is fine.
